Government Information

Our Village is a "Home Rule" Community governed by the Illinois Constitution. Our Village ordinances can be seen here. Since the Village is so small, we've had a volunteer government for most of our history. The Village Board meets on the second Monday of every month at the Village Hall (although in this current Covid-19 environment, virtually) and Meeting Agendas can be found on this site or posted in the Village Post Office. Each of our six Trustees has separate responsibilities and functions like department heads while our Village Administrator, Michelle Shapiro, handles most of the day to day work of the Village.
