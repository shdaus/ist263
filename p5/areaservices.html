Village Post Hours

Our Village Post Office is open from 8:30 to 11:30 am and 12:30 to 5:00 pm Monday thru Friday and their phone number is 847-729-1666.

Metra Parking

Metra parking cost $2 per day and is not assigned. It is allocated only on a first-come, first-serve basis each day. There is NO Metra parking south of the Post Office or anywhere else in the Village of Golf. Also, there is NO monthly Metra parking.
